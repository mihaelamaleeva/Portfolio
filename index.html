Bike-Sharing Company Analysis of Customer Behaviour

This analysis includes the data from Divvy bike-sharing company in Chicago. The main goal is to decide how to make casual cyclists that use the service just for the day to buy an annual membership. The focus is how these two types of customers use the service in order to determine how to accomplish the goal.

In this R project everything from importing the data to exporting it is included. Every step of the cleaning and transforming the data is written to maintain the integrity of the data. The only thing that was done manually is downloading the data and importing the separate files in Kaggle dataset, as the source website was protected.
